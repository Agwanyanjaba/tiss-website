Our Approach

TISS operates at the intersection of three distinct domains:

  • Research: We conduct independent, interdisciplinary inquiry that pushes beyond conventional wisdom. Our outputs include policy briefs, white papers, scenario analyses, and longitudinal studies designed for decision-makers.
  • Engagement: We facilitate strategic dialogues, roundtables, and executive briefings that connect researchers with practitioners in settings conducive to candor and collaboration.
  • Impact: We measure our success not by volume of publications alone, but by the extent to which our insights inform policy, shape discourse, and contribute to tangible outcomes in governance and security.

Synthesis: Together, the six pillars form an integrated framework with a logical progression:

Pillar Sequential Flow Core Function
Economic Governance & Fiscal Architecture Diagnostics → Decentralization → PFM → Auditing → Sustainable Governance Establishes the fiscal systems and accountability mechanisms that enable effective public action
Social Policy & Equity Analysis Mapping → Expenditure Tracking → Medical Studies → Engineering Ensures that action is targeted toward those most in need through interdisciplinary solutions spanning health and infrastructure
Evidence-Based Policy & Institutional Learning Impact Evaluation → Big Data → Shocks Analysis Ensures that action is continuously improved through rigorous feedback and resilience planning
