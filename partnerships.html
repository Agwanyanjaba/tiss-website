Strategic Partnerships

TISS recognizes that the challenges of our time exceed the capacity of any single institution. We actively cultivate partnerships with:

  • Academic and research institutions to advance interdisciplinary inquiry.
  • Government agencies to ensure research relevance and policy alignment.
  • Private sector organizations to integrate economic perspectives into strategic analysis.
  • Civil society groups to incorporate diverse voices into our dialogues.

Who We Serve

TISS is a resource for leaders who bear the weight of strategic decisions. Our primary stakeholders include:

  • Government Leaders: Providing scenario-based intelligence and policy options for national and regional security challenges.
  • Corporate Executives: Delivering geopolitical risk assessments and strategic foresight to inform business resilience and expansion.
  • Academic Institutions: Collaborating on interdisciplinary research initiatives and serving as a bridge between academia and policymaking.
  • Multilateral Organizations: Contributing expertise on governance, security architecture, and regional stability.

Our Commitment to Independence

TISS is an independent, non-partisan institution. Our analysis is guided solely by evidence, intellectual integrity, and a commitment to the public good. We do not advocate for predetermined outcomes; rather, we equip our partners with the tools to arrive at their own informed decisions. This independence is the foundation of our credibility and the source of the trust we enjoy across the political and institutional spectrum.

Looking Forward

The Tiordekuei Institute for Strategic Studies is built for the long horizon. As the global landscape continues to evolve, TISS remains committed to its founding purpose: to provide the foresight, analysis, and platforms leaders need to act with wisdom and courage. We invite partners, scholars, and decision-makers to join us in this essential work—where research informs policy, where dialogue fosters understanding, and where the architects of change converge to shape a more secure and equitable future.
