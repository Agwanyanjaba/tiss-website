Overview

In an era defined by volatility, complexity, and rapid geopolitical realignment, the need for institutions that combine intellectual rigor with practical relevance has never been more urgent. The Tiordekuei Institute for Strategic Studies (TISS) Ltd exists to meet this need. As a registered think tank and research institution, TISS is dedicated to the pursuit of strategic clarity — transforming the uncertainty of a shifting World order into actionable intelligence for policymakers entrusted with leadership, academia, and governance.
